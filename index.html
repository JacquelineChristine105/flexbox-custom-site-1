Welcome!

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs and books, I can be found traveling, hiking and searching for new stories.

I was born and raised in Northern Alberta, Canada and here my roots run very deep! My heart and soul belongs to the wide-spread forests here, open prairies and blue hills. Alberta will always be my home--even though I reside in Tulum from time to time. I have a flair for travel but my heart will always belong to my home, my husband, and my kitties!

My passions all involve pushing the bounds of technology and my volunteer to work with animals. I spend a lot of time doing concentrated work and study--I love learning!!--and nature is always my release back to myself. I'm slowly learning to be more connected, still and present in this ever busy world and it has been a true joy every step of the way.

Fun Fact: I love teaching others! Becoming a teacher has been on my heart for a number of years, but I never knew what exactly I wanted to teach. I'm currently working on a course (of which I CANNOT WAIT to release in 2022) all about the power of finding your passion through conecting to your spiritual center. It's honestly so much fun!
